.strategy

Plan. Execute. Learn. Iterate.
O

Plan

The first step to amazing work is learning my clients' needs, wants and styles. We start the process by discussing project goals, benchmarks, and a schedule for execution.

Execute

Once visual styles and project objectives are established, I work in short sprints, starting with the basic structure and working up to the finer details. I meet with clients at the end of each sprint to make sure the project is going in the right direction.

Learn

For clients that have a specific business goal in mind, we get real world data to determine if the current design meets their needs. From page views to form submissions, the data will guide the next steps.

Iterate

Based off of the information we collect from the site, we decide next steps to move towards the ultimate business goal.
